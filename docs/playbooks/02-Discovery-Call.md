# Playbook 02: Discovery Call (60 minutes)

Goal: turn "we need software" into a clear MVP plan with scope, risks, and access needs.

## Before the call (10 minutes)

- Confirm decision maker is attending
- Get their website + Google Business Profile link
- Ask what system they use today (CRM, DMS, WhatsApp, spreadsheets)
- Prepare 3 likely MVP options to suggest:
  - Reservation + deposit + receipt + approvals
  - Service booking portal + photo uploads + status updates
  - Vehicle Passport (docs, media, handover evidence)

## Call agenda (60 minutes)

### 0 to 5 min: Set the frame

- "I will ask workflow questions, then I will summarize an MVP and timeline."
- "We will also confirm who approves decisions and what must be recorded for disputes."

### 5 to 20 min: Pain and money

Ask:

- Where do you lose time daily
- Where do you lose money monthly
- Where do disputes happen (deposits, handover, service issues)
- What is the top 1 outcome you want in 30 days

Write down exact examples they mention.

### 20 to 35 min: Workflow mapping

Ask them to walk you through:
Lead -> reservation -> deposit -> approval -> invoice -> delivery -> aftersales

Mark:

- Who approves what
- What evidence exists today (screenshots, WhatsApp, paper)
- What must be saved for disputes

### 35 to 45 min: Systems and integrations

Ask:

- Website platform (Next, WordPress, custom)
- CRM name
- DMS name
- Payment provider
- Email provider
- WhatsApp usage (Business app or API)

Decide:

- MVP will be standalone first or integrated from day 1

### 45 to 55 min: MVP decision

Say:

- "Based on what you said, the MVP should be X because it reduces Y risk and saves Z effort."

Lock:

- 5 features max
- 2 roles max for MVP (admin + staff, customer later if needed)
- Timeline and milestone structure

### 55 to 60 min: Next steps

Confirm:

- Who signs off scope
- Who provides access
- Deposit timing
- Next meeting date for prototype demo

## Output after the call (send within 2 hours)

Create and send:

1. MVP summary (1 page)

- Goals (3 bullets)
- MVP features (max 5)
- What is excluded (phase 2 list)
- Timeline (weeks)
- Milestones + payment plan
- Access needed list

2. Risks and assumptions

- Example: "If CRM has no API, we start without integration."
